Profile

Walsh Group

Walsh Group is an innovative Ballarat based design, construction and development company.

The unique blend of interior design, construction and development credentials of the company provides customers with the comfort they are receiving the best design advice.

We closely monitor international trends while infusing our own creative design talents and knowledge of the Australian context into our designs.

Georgina has a highly attuned knowledge of design and customer needs and draws on experience in working across a broad range of fields.

Greg has an extensive background as a builder and has well-honed his craftsmanship and design skills over 30 years in different trades.

Our build team are energetic, honest with an eye for detail and strive to deliver great customer service with great experience.
We believe that by working closely with you and your needs we can deliver better end results, that will not only last the test of time as far as style goes but also the heavy work load of daily life.
